Our Journey

We believe there is no replacement of home food and food need not be restricted to cooking ourselves or eating from restaurants. We aim at redefining mealtimes at home and you can now eat exotic, genuine cuisine that is made fresh and healthy in kitchens like yours. This idea is a thought to serve people with wide restaurant kind of cuisine, but cooked in a traditional home kitchen, we recommend you to visit our place to see your food cooked.

Why order from us

  • Fresh food - We begin to procure the ingredients and prepare the meals only after an order is placed, means you get the freshest possible food.

  • Healthy - We have high standards for cleanliness and hygiene. We feed our families from the same kitchen using similar ingredients. Therefore you will always be guaranteed a good, fresh and healthy meal.

  • Genuine - We are doing this because we are passionate about cooking and we love to cook. We are not restaurant cooks who are just doing their jobs to earn their daily wages.

  • Customized - We are willing to listen to the foodie and customize to the tastes-like less oil, less heat, less spicy etc.
